Welcome to the website of the Society of Informatics and Computer Sciences.

This year our annual forum will be in Bordeaux (France), from 5th to 7th October 2022.

Society of Informatics and Computer Sciences' forum is the largest international conference about computer technology.
The forum scientific program will cover all domains in modern research and industry.
